Overview

About the main rules

This game is pretty simple:

  1. You need to choose between many heroes whose have different roles and habilities.
  2. You are in a group of 5 players againts another team.
  3. You need to destroy the ennemy core.
  4. You can gain experience in game for your team by taking objectives, killing minions and killing ennemy team.
  5. You also have different battleground which obey to different rules.
  6. You can complete daily quest that will give you gold and chest to earn some cool prizes.

Heroes roles

In this game you can play 70 heroes. You must chose between 4 classes.

The assassin class:

  • Can deal a hight amount of damage to one target or more.
  • Some of them have a great mobility.
  • Work in synergie with warrior classes.
  • They are fragile, with few defensive abilities.
  • Some of them are really dependant of their mana.

The warrior class:

  • They can take many damage.
  • Some of them can heal themselves.
  • They also have great offensive capability.
  • But they can't handle other enemy heroes alone.

The support class:

  • They have a strong healing.
  • They have some control and enemy disruption through roots and stuns.
  • They can't be alone on the battle field due to their lack of damage.

The specialist class:

  • They have strong habilities to push the lane.
  • They can easily take objectives.
  • They don't have many habitlies to escape from a dangerous situation.
  • They are often out the teamfight to do a camp or taking a turret.
  • Some of them are pretty complicated.

Game Mode

You can find several game mode in this game.
In the VS A.I mode,you can eather play against or with A.I to learn and practice.
In the quick match mode, you can compete against 5 other player with the same skill as you.
In the Unrakedmode, you can build a team with other player, based on the battleground and the enemy team.
In the Rankedmode, you can do the same thing as the unranked mode, but with a rank.At the end of the season of the game, you can earn great prizes.
